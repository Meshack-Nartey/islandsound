Four features. Zero clutter.

Mood-Based Dynamic Theming

The glow shifts in real time with the music — electric indigo for high energy, warm gold for worship, soft amber when things calm down. All computed on-device with FFT, no internet required.

Cross-App Listening Continuity

Hand a track off between Apple Music, Spotify, Boomplay and YouTube Music — same song, same position, different app.

Listen Together

Start or join a room with a six-character code and stay perfectly in sync with friends — playback, reactions and all.

“Hey Island” Voice Control

Skip, pause, change the volume, or ask “who sings this?” — hands-free, on-device, with ShazamKit-powered song ID.
